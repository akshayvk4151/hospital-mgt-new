About Us

National Hospital, one of the earliest hospitals in Malabar, has been in service to the cause of health for nearly half a century. This 400 bedded super-specialty
hospital, most strategically situated in the heart of the city at Mavoor Road junction, Kozhikode, is well equipped to handle medical emergencies and is considered
to be one of the best referral hospitals in the region. The easy accessibility to the hospital by road, rail and air and the team of well qualified and dedicated doctors
and paramedical staff make it a preferred health care destination.
